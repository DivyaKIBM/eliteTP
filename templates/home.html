Leave Travel Allowance (LTA) Policy Details

Eligibility

Leave (Vacation Time off) must be applied and approved in Workday for the period he/she is raising the claim under LTA. The expenses incurred should be of personal nature and not relating to a business trip.

Amount

Full round-trip cost of the chosen mode of transportation for eligible parties traveling (shortest route to the destination must be taken)

Qualifying expenses

Valid for domestic travel, two times every 4 calendar years. Expenses incurred for 'travel' only are considered - example: Air fares, Railway fares or bus / public transport fares. Any other expenses incurred towards local conveyance, taxi charges, food, accommodation, driver's bata, porter charges, tips, sightseeing, travel insurance etc. are not allowed as part of LTA.
